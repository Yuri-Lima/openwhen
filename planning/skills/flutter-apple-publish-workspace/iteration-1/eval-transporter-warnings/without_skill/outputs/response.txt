WHY THESE CHANGES WORK
----------------------
- Removing "remote-notification" tells Apple your app doesn't use remote push,
  so it won't require push-to-talk entitlements.

- Adding NSLocationAlwaysUsageDescription ensures compliance with Apple's
  privacy policy requirements for location access across all iOS versions.

- The combination of NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription +
  NSLocationAlwaysUsageDescription covers all supported iOS versions and
  user permission scenarios.
